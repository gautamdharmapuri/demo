Bowling Is Fun!

You Know What? We’re All Bowlers. You, your neighbor, your boss, your best friend, even your high school principal. Whether it’s only a couple frames a year, a memorable bowling birthday party from childhood, a friendly bowling league or a weekly way to unwind; everyone bowls. At Spare Time Entertainment Center of Lansing, we always have a bowling lane reserved for the bowler in you. Reserve your lane now.

Laser Tag and Arcade

Spare Time is SO much more than a bowling alley! We also offer an awesome 2-story laser tag game facility and a 4000+ foot redemption arcade with tons of cool games including a Laser Maze! Stop in and let loose with any of our fun attractions.

Grand River Bar and Grill

Dine with us tonight at the Grand River Bar and Grill.   We offer a full service sit down restaurant in Lansing with healthy dining options, as well as your favorite bar food and bowling food.  A fast paced game of laser tag or Mario Kart can sure work up an appetite, so grab the gang and come dine with us today.
